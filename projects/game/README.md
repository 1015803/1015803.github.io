  2 This game is a somewhat historically acurate 1900s Japan game after the great unification under Lord Nobunaga. Your goal in the game is to travel across 6 cities defeating other warriors and training to eventually defeat lord nobunaga in as few days as possible. every move you make however takes up a day. To beat an opponent you must have morew power than them. To obtain power you must either train or defeat other opponents.
