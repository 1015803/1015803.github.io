Scotish Shortbread

This is my family shortbread recipe. It its a family recipe created by my great great grandmother in scotland and has been passed through generations. This recipe has been a family tradition to make for holidays and birthdays on my moms side of the family and making it with my grandparents is one of my first memories.

Recipe

Time
Cook Time: 40 mins
Prep Time: 5 mins
Total Time: 45 mins

For this recipe you will need

  • 1 cup of suggar
  • 1 pound of butter
  • 1 cup of rice flour
  • 4 cups of flour

Cooking

  1. Put all ingrediants into a large mixing bowl
  2. Knead the mixture untill fully mixed
  3. Press the dough firmly into an ungreased 9x13-inch pan, ensuring the surface is smooth.
  4. Use a fork to prick the dough all over, which prevents it from puffing up while baking.
  5. Sprinkle with extra sugar if desired.
  6. Bake at 300°F (150°C) for 35-40 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden brown.
  7. While still warm, cut the shortbread into squares or fingers with a sharp knife.
  8. Let the shortbread cool completely in the pan before removing it.
